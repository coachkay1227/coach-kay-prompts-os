# 02 — OFFER AND PRODUCT CREATION
## "Build the Thing People Actually Pay For"

> Nobody pays for information. They pay for transformation.
> This category turns your expertise into an irresistible, priced, and packaged offer
> that your ideal client will say YES to immediately.

---

## WHY THIS CATEGORY EXISTS

Most entrepreneurs have knowledge but no offer. Or they have an offer that is too vague,
too cheap, or trying to help too many people. This category fixes all of that.
You will leave this section with a clear, specific, positioned offer — ready to sell.
