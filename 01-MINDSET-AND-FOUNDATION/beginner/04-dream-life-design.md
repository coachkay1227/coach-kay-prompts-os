# DREAM LIFE DESIGN — Define What You Are Actually Building Toward
## Category: 01 - Mindset and Foundation | Level: BEGINNER

---

## WHAT THIS PROMPT DOES
Forces you to get specific about the life you want so you build a business that serves it —
not a business that owns you. Most entrepreneurs build for 5 years and arrive somewhere
they never wanted to be. This prompt prevents that.

## STANDALONE USE
Use this to: Create a vision board script, write your "about me" story, build a life planning
module for your coaching program, start a journaling practice, or re-anchor during hard seasons.

## THE PROMPT

```
You are a visionary life design coach with expertise in helping high-achievers
create a vivid, specific picture of their ideal life — and then reverse-engineer
a business model that funds and enables that life.

You believe the business should serve the life, not the other way around.
You are known for asking questions that make people cry and laugh at the same time
because you see what they truly want before they can name it.

Here is where I am right now:
- Current life situation: [describe your work, living situation, income, relationships]
- What I am most unhappy or unfulfilled about right now: [be honest]
- What freedom means to me: [describe it specifically]
- My non-negotiables (things I refuse to give up): [list them]
- My income goal and what I want to do with that money: [be specific]
- How I want to spend a perfect Tuesday (no obligations): [describe in detail]

Now design my dream life:

1. THE VISION — 3 YEARS FROM NOW
   Write a vivid, first-person, present-tense narrative of my life 3 years from now.
   Include: where I wake up, how my morning starts, the work I do and how much,
   how much I earn and from what, my relationships, my health, my impact, how I feel.
   Make it so specific and real I can feel it.
   Minimum 200 words. No generic language.

2. THE FREEDOM NUMBER
   Based on my dream life vision, calculate my Freedom Number —
   the monthly income that funds this life completely.
   Break it down: fixed costs + lifestyle costs + savings + investment + giving.

3. THE BUSINESS THAT FUNDS THIS LIFE
   Given my Freedom Number and my non-negotiables,
   what type of business model makes the most sense?
   Give me 2-3 options with pros and cons for each relative to MY stated life goals.

4. THE 90-DAY NORTH STAR
   Write one powerful sentence that captures where I am going in the next 90 days.
   This becomes the filter for every decision I make: "Does this move me toward my North Star?"

5. THE DAILY ALIGNMENT CHECK
   Give me 3 questions I can ask myself every morning to stay aligned with this vision
   and not get distracted by other people's urgency.

Be vivid, specific, and emotionally resonant. This should feel like a movie trailer for my future.
```

---

## HOW TO USE THIS PROMPT
1. The vision paragraph you get back — save it, read it every morning
2. Calculate your actual Freedom Number and write it somewhere visible
3. Use the 90-Day North Star sentence as your email signature, screensaver, or mirror note
4. Run this prompt every 90 days — your dream evolves as you grow

## THE .1% SECRET
Successful entrepreneurs do not work harder. They work from a clearer picture.
The more specific your vision, the more your brain works on it while you sleep.
This is not woo — it is neuroscience. Your reticular activating system filters
for what you focus on. Give it something specific to find.

## ITERATION PROMPTS
- "The vision you wrote is beautiful but feels out of reach. Help me write a 6-month version that feels scary but achievable."
- "Turn my dream life vision into a 60-second voice memo script I can record and listen to every morning."
- "Write a 'Day in My Life' Instagram or TikTok script based on my dream life vision — positioning it as what I am building toward."
